Connecting local farmers with families who value fresh food.

KisanToGrahak is a farmer-to-customer marketplace built to make fresh vegetables, fruits, grains, and daily produce easier to discover, order, and sell. The platform helps customers buy directly from trusted local farmers while giving farmers a simple digital space to list products, manage stock, and receive orders.

Fresh, transparent, and farmer-first

Customers can compare available products, view farmer details, add items to cart, and place orders with clear quantity and price information.

Our Purpose

Why KisanToGrahak Exists

We are reducing the distance between farms and homes by making product discovery, ordering, and farmer management simple for everyone involved.

For Farmers

Farmers can create their profile, list products from the master catalogue, set prices, update stock, and manage customer orders from one place.

For Customers

Customers can search products, compare farmers, understand availability, and purchase farm-fresh produce with a straightforward cart and checkout flow.

For Admins

Admins can manage categories, product masters, roles, and platform records so the marketplace stays organized, reliable, and easy to scale.

What We Support

A cleaner way to buy and sell local produce.
