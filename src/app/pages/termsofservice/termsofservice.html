Guidelines for using the KisanToGrahak marketplace responsibly.

These terms describe the expected use of KisanToGrahak by customers, farmers, and administrators. They help keep product listings, orders, and marketplace activity clear and fair.

Use the platform fairly

KisanToGrahak works best when farmers provide accurate product information and customers place genuine orders with correct contact and delivery details.

Effective date: May 18, 2026
1. Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for the information entered during registration and for keeping their login details secure. Accounts should not be used to submit false details, fake orders, or misleading product data.

2. Farmer Listings

Farmers should list only products they can reasonably supply. Product names, prices, images, availability, and quantity should be kept accurate so customers can make informed decisions.

3. Customer Orders

Customers should review product, farmer, price, and quantity details before checkout. Once an order is placed, the relevant farmer may use the order details to prepare and fulfill the request.

4. Admin Controls

Administrators may manage roles, categories, product masters, and platform records to keep KisanToGrahak organized. Admin actions should support fair usage and reliable marketplace operations.

5. Acceptable Use

Users should not misuse the platform, attempt unauthorized access, upload harmful content, interfere with order processing, or use the service in a way that harms farmers, customers, or administrators.

6. Updates to Terms

These terms may change as new marketplace features are added. Continued use of the platform means users agree to follow the latest version shown on this page.
