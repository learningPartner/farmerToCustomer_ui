Process

How FarmConnect Works

Three simple steps connecting farmers and customers directly, no intermediaries involved.

1. Farmer Registers & Lists

Farmers register and list the produce they plan to sell — from vegetables to grains, with quantity and price details.

2. Customer Searches & Orders

Customers search for any product, see all available farmers, and place orders specifying quantity in kg or bunches.

3. Order Goes to Farmer

Each order is routed directly to the respective farmer, just like platform marketplaces — orders go to the right vendor automatically.
