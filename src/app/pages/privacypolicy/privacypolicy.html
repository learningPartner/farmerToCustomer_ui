Privacy Policy

How KisanToGrahak respects and protects user information.

This policy explains what information our farmer-to-customer platform may collect, why it is used, and how we aim to keep customer, farmer, and admin data handled responsibly.

Simple Summary

We use information to create accounts, show products, process orders, support users, and improve the marketplace experience. We do not design this platform to sell personal data.

Last updated: May 18, 2026
1. Information We Collect

KisanToGrahak may collect account details such as name, phone number, email address, role, address, and login information. For farmers, we may also store profile details, product listings, stock quantities, pricing, and order history.

2. How We Use Information

We use data to register users, display farmer products, manage carts, place and track orders, maintain category and product records, provide support, and improve platform reliability.

3. Order and Delivery Details

Customer order details may be shared with the relevant farmer so the order can be prepared, confirmed, and delivered. Farmers should use customer information only for fulfilling platform orders.

4. Data Security

We aim to protect platform data through role-based access, controlled admin workflows, and responsible handling of account and order records. Users should keep their passwords private and log out on shared devices.

5. User Choices

Users may update account information where the platform allows it. If account correction or removal is needed, users can contact platform support or the administrator managing the KisanToGrahak deployment.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this policy as the application grows. Any important changes should be reflected on this page so users understand how their information is handled.
