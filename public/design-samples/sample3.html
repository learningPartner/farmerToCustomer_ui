Simple Process

How KisanToGrahak Works

From farm listing to your doorstep in 4 transparent steps.

01
🛡️
Admin Creates Catalogue

Super admin sets up categories and a master product list. This ensures standardized product naming across all farmers.

02
🌾
Farmer Lists Products

Registered farmers choose products from the catalogue and list what they'll have available — with price and quantity in kg.

03
🔍
Customer Searches

Customer searches any product, sees all farmers who have it listed, and adds their preferred quantity to the cart.

04
📦
Orders Auto-Split

One cart, multiple farmers. Orders are automatically split and sent to each respective farmer — exactly like Amazon's marketplace.

Platform Roles

Three Roles, One Platform

Super Admin

The platform controller who sets up the master catalogue and governs the entire ecosystem.

  • Create product categories and catalogue
  • Approve farmer registrations
  • Monitor all platform orders
  • Manage user roles and permissions
  • View platform analytics
Farmer

Farmers register on the platform and list what they're growing this season to connect with buyers.

  • Register and create seller profile
  • List products from admin catalogue
  • Set price and available quantity
  • Receive direct order notifications
  • Track all sales and manage stock
Customer

Customers search for fresh produce, see multiple farmers, and order directly at fair prices.

  • Browse and search all products
  • See all farmers offering same product
  • Add items from multiple farmers to cart
  • Orders split automatically per farmer
  • Track each order's status separately
