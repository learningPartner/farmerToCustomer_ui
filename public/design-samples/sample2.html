The Process

How It Works

A transparent, direct connection between the farm and your table.

Step 01
🛡️
Admin Sets Up Catalogue

Super admin creates product categories and a master product list. This becomes the standard catalogue all farmers use.

Step 02
🌾
Farmer Lists Products

Farmers register, pick products they grow from the catalogue, add their price and available stock for upcoming seasons.

Step 03
🔍
Customer Searches

Customer searches "potato" and sees all farmers listing it. They choose, add to cart with desired quantity in kg.

Step 04
📦
Orders Split Automatically

Multi-farmer cart orders are split automatically. Each farmer receives only their portion — like Amazon's multi-vendor system.

Platform Roles

Who Uses FarmConnect?

Super Admin

Platform controller

  • Create & manage product categories
  • Define master product catalogue
  • Approve/reject farmer registrations
  • Monitor all platform activity
  • Manage all user roles
Farmer

Product seller

  • Register & verify farm profile
  • List products from admin catalogue
  • Set prices and stock quantity
  • Receive & manage orders
  • Track sales and inventory
Customer

Buyer & consumer

  • Browse & search products
  • Compare farmers for same product
  • Add items from multiple farmers
  • Orders auto-split per farmer
  • Track order status real-time
